Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Here's how we protect it.

Our Commitment: We don't collect, store, or share your personal data. All our apps are designed to work with complete privacy.

Effective Date

This privacy policy is effective as of 18th March, 2026, and applies to all Aretivo apps.

No Data Collection

We do not collect any personal information from our users. Our apps are designed to respect your privacy from the ground up:

  • No account creation required
  • No data transmitted to our servers (we do not operate any backend services)
  • No location data collection
  • No contact or identity information gathered

Local Processing Only

Some features of the app, such as music playback statistics and library updates, involve analyzing audio files and usage activity on your device.

  • This processing happens entirely locally on your device
  • No data is collected, stored externally, or shared with any third parties

Music Player Specific Policy

Aulosyne follows these privacy principles:

  • The app only scans your device's local storage to find audio files for playback
  • All music playback happens locally on your device
  • No music files are uploaded or transmitted anywhere
  • Playback history, statistics, and preferences are stored locally and never leave your device
  • No internet connection is required for basic functionality

100% Offline Operation

All Aretivo apps are designed to work without an internet connection:

  • No cloud services or remote servers
  • No background data usage
  • No telemetry or crash reporting
  • No automatic updates fetching

Your data remains exclusively on your device where it belongs.

No Third-Party Services

We do not integrate with any third-party services that could compromise your privacy:

  • No advertising networks or SDKs
  • No analytics platforms (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • No social media integration
  • No tracking cookies or web beacons
  • No data sharing with partners or affiliates

Security

Since our apps don't collect or transmit any personal data:

  • There is no personal data at risk of being compromised
  • Your files and media remain private on your device
  • No data breaches are possible because we don't store user data
  • App permissions are minimal and only for core functionality

App Permissions

We request only the permissions necessary for our apps to function:

Music Player: Storage access (for local files only)

Permissions are never used to collect data beyond what's needed for the app's core functionality.

Policy Updates

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will:

  • Update the effective date at the top of this page
  • Summarize changes in app update notes
  • Maintain our commitment to not collecting user data

We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we protect your privacy.

Children's Privacy

Our apps are suitable for users of all ages because:

  • We don't collect any personal information from anyone
  • No age verification is required
  • No targeted content based on age or interests
  • No social features that could expose children to risks
